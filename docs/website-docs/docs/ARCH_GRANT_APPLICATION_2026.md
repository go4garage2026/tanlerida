### 20. What problem does your product or service address?

**1. The Discovery Problem**: Premium leather goods are deeply personal products—they must match a buyer's body type, skin tone, style sensibility, and occasion. Yet most e-commerce platforms treat leather goods like commodities, offering only generic category filters and static product photos. This results in high return rates (25–40% in online fashion), low customer confidence, and abandoned carts. Customers literally don't know which product is *right for them*.

**2. The Access Problem**: India produces some of the world's finest leather goods—the country exported $5.7 billion in leather products in FY 2024–25—yet American consumers have almost no direct access to Indian premium leather brands. The supply chain is dominated by intermediaries, markups compound 3–5x, and Indian artisan craftsmanship is invisible behind Western brand labels.

Tangred solves both problems simultaneously: **Tan Lerida™** eliminates guesswork with AI-powered personal styling, while our **direct-to-consumer model** delivers Indian artisan leather at 40–60% below comparable Western luxury brands.

### 21. Describe your product or service.

**Tangred** is a full-stack e-commerce platform and premium leather brand with two interconnected offerings:

**1. The Product Line — Premium Indian Leather Goods**

We offer a curated collection of handcrafted leather products across six categories: Office Bags, Belts, Jackets, Wallets, Briefcases, and Accessories. Every product is made from premium materials—full-grain leather, Nappa leather, vegetable-tanned leather, and crazy horse leather—sourced from India's leather corridor. Products range from ₹1,299 ($16) to ₹24,999 ($300), with lead times of 5–21 working days for made-to-order pieces.

Each product features multiple variants (color, size, finish) and is photographed in a luxury editorial style consistent with our dark, cinematic brand identity.

**2. The Technology — Tan Lerida™ AI Styling Assistant**

Tan Lerida™ is our proprietary AI personal styling engine, offered as a paid consultation service (₹116.82 / ~$1.40 per session) that delivers a highly personalized product recommendation. The pipeline works as follows:

- **Step 1 — Photo Upload**: The customer uploads 3–4 photos (casual, formal, full body, ethnic) via our drag-and-drop interface with Cloudinary CDN integration.
- **Step 2 — Body Profile**: The customer provides anthropometric data (gender, age range, height, body build, skin tone) and selects style resonance markers (Classic Professional, Contemporary Smart, Traditional Indian, Minimalist, Bold Statement).
- **Step 3 — Style Preferences**: The customer describes their need, budget range, occasion, laptop requirements, and color preferences in natural language.
- **Step 4 — AI Analysis & Recommendation**: Our orchestration pipeline runs:
  - **Google Gemini Vision** analyzes the uploaded photos to detect body shape, undertone, posture, face shape, and color bias.
  - **Pinecone Vector Search** matches the user's profile against our product catalog using semantic embeddings (text-embedding-004).
  - **Anthropic Claude** generates a narrative-rich recommendation with a primary product pick, alternatives, and a craft story—all in the voice of "Tan Lerida," our AI master tailor persona.
  - **Stability AI** generates a photorealistic outfit visualization showing the customer wearing the recommended product.
- **Step 5 — Delivery**: The customer receives their personalized recommendation, AI-generated visualization, and an estimated delivery date—all within their account dashboard.

**The Technology Stack**

The platform is built on Next.js 16 (App Router), React 19, TypeScript 5, Prisma 7 with PostgreSQL, and deployed as a containerized standalone server on Railway. The current repository includes 32 API route files, 20+ shared library modules, Prisma-backed commerce data models, Zustand state management, Framer Motion interactions, and a dark luxury UI system designed for premium positioning.
